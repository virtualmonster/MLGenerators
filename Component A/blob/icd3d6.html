2023 Continuous Testing Trends Report

In the fall of 2023, HCL Software sent a survey to more than 5,000 professionals working in a wide variety of positions, industries and locations around the world.

The insights documented in this trends report are useful to better understand the testing practices, tools and trends that are being adopted by developers in 2023. The data provides companies with transparency of the challenges and successes their peers face in the world of application testing.

With digital transformation paving the way for future innovations, AI and ML continue to be the front-runners for changing the way software is developed, deployed and ultimately improving operational efficiency. With the feedback from software engineers and industry professionals we are able to understand the continuous trends that are paving the way for DevOps.
